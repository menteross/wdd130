Nairobi

Nairobi the capital of Kenya and "Green City in the Sun.", is a unique metropolis. It stands out globally as the only major city with a national park right on its doorstep. Nairobi National Park allows visitors to see lions, rhinos, and giraffes roaming against a backdrop of towering skyscrapers, perfectly capturing the city's distinct blend of wilderness and urban development.
