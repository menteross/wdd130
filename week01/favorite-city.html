Favorite City

Nairobi the capital of Kenya and "Green City in the Sun.", is a unique metropolis. It stands out globally as the only major city with a national park right on its doorstep. Nairobi National Park allows visitors to see lions, rhinos, and giraffes roaming against a backdrop of towering skyscrapers, perfectly capturing the city's distinct blend of wilderness and urban development.

Nairobi serves as the economic, political, and cultural heartbeat of East Africa. It is a thriving hub for technology and innovation, often referred to as the "Silicon Savannah". It hosts major international hubs, including the United Nations African headquarters. Nairobi is a dynamic gateway that beautifully bridges Kenya's rich heritage with its forward-looking future.
